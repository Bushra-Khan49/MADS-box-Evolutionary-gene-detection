# Step 1. Data Collection — (*Glycine max*)

**Aim**  
To download Whole Genome, Whole Proteome, CDS, and annotations data for the paleopolyploid eudicot *Glycine max* (Soybean) from Phytozome v2.1.

---

### Why *Glycine max*?
*Glycine max* is a major legume crop and a representative of the Fabaceae family. It is a paleopolyploid species that has undergone two rounds of whole-genome duplication (WGD) approximately 59 and 13 million years ago. This makes it an essential model for studying gene family expansion, subfunctionalization, and redundancy in MADS-box genes after polyploidy events. The Phytozome v2.1 assembly provides a highly refined reference for this complex genome.

---

### Tasks
- Identify the *Glycine max* v2.1 assembly on Phytozome (DOE JGI).
- Download the complete protein dataset for genome-wide MADS-box search.
- Retrieve the corresponding GFF3 and CDS files for gene model characterization.

### Databases Used
- **Phytozome (DOE JGI)** → Primary source for *Glycine max* genomic data.
  - [https://phytozome-next.jgi.doe.gov](https://phytozome-next.jgi.doe.gov)
- **Pfam** → Domain HMM profiles (PF00319, PF01486).

### File Formats
| Data | File Type | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Proteome | `Gmax_275_v2.1.protein.fa` | Main search database |
| Genome | `Gmax_275_v2.1.genome.fa` | Genomic mapping |
| Annotation | `Gmax_275_v2.1.gene.gff3` | Site coordinates |

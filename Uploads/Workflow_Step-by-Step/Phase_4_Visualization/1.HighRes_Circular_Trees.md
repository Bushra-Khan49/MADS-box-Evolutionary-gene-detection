> ### Step 2: Key Algorithms Utilized in Script
* **Nearest-Neighbor Imputation Algorithm:** `draw_colored_trees.py` dynamically computes minimum branch distances between unmapped *Arabidopsis* identifiers (`AT5G26880`...) to inherently copy the nearest anchored Subclade hex-code without human intervention!
* **Opacity Shading:** The Python dictionary fades the *Arabidopsis* Hex colors natively by `65%` through dynamic rgb/white blending, enforcing absolute visual precedence exclusively to our 9 Target Species nodes. 
* **Topological Re-Alignment:** `ts.force_topology = False` preserves mathematical branches while `position="aligned"` mechanically drops guiding-dashed-lines out to a mathematically clean text-ring edge.

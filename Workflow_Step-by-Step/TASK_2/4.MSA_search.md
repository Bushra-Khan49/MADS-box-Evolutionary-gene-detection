> ### Step 4: Multiple Sequence Alignment (MSA) — (*Amborella trichopoda*)

**Aim**  
To align identified *Amborella trichopoda* MADS-box protein sequences for phylogenetic context and ancestral clade mapping.

---

### Command-Line Alignment (CLI)
```bash
cd ~/Desktop/NIPGR-data/NIPGR_WORK/MSA_project

# Perform alignment using MAFFT
mafft --maxiterate 1000 --localpair Amborella_trichopoda_candidates.fa > Aligned_Amborella_trichopoda.fa
```
[Amborella MSA Alignment](https://github.com/Bushra-Khan49/MADS-box-Evolutionary-gene-detection/tree/main/Workflow_Step-by-Step/TASK_2/uploads)

---

### MEGA 12 Alignment
I performed the alignment in **MEGA 12** as follows:
1.  Open **MEGA 12** and click **Align** -> **Edit/Build Alignment**.
2.  Choose **Create a new alignment** -> **Protein**.
3.  Open `Amborella_trichopoda_candidates.fa` in the alignment explorer.
4.  Select all sequences (**Ctrl+A**) then click **Alignment** -> **Align by MUSCLE**.
5.  Save the alignment as `Aligned_Amborella_trichopoda.meg`.

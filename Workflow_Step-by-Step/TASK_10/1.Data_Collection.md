# Step 1. Data Collection — (*Nelumbo nucifera*)

**Aim**  
To download Whole Genome, Whole Proteome, CDS, and annotations data for the basal eudicot *Nelumbo nucifera* (Sacred Lotus) from NCBI RefSeq (v1.0).

---

### Why *Nelumbo nucifera*?
*Nelumbo nucifera* (Sacred Lotus) is a basal eudicot and an important phylogenetic anchor for understanding the transition from basal angiosperms to core eudicots. It possesses a relatively small genome that has avoided the recent whole-genome duplications seen in other aquatic eudicots. Studying the MADS-box gene family in *N. nucifera* (NCBI RefSeq GCF_000365185.1) is essential for tracing the ancestral state of floral organ identity genes before the radiation of core eudicots.

---

### Tasks
- Identify the *Nelumbo nucifera* v1.0 assembly on NCBI RefSeq.
- Download the complete protein dataset for genome-wide MADS-box search.
- Retrieve the corresponding GFF3 and CDS files for gene model characterization.

### Databases Used
- **NCBI RefSeq** → Primary source for *Nelumbo nucifera* genomic data.
  - [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/refseq/](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/refseq/)
- **UniProt** → Secondary source for protein validation.
- **Pfam** → Domain HMM profiles (PF00319, PF01486).

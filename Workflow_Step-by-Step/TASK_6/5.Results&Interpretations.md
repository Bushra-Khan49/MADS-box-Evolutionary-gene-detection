### Clade Classification Overview
Due to its history of two whole-genome duplications (WGDs), *Glycine max* exhibits significant expansions in almost every MADS-box clade compared to *Arabidopsis*. This redundancy suggests extensive subfunctionalization or neofunctionalization of floral organ identity genes.

### Phylogenetic Validation
I constructed a Maximum Likelihood (ML) tree in MEGA 12, incorporating all 228 validated candidates along with *Arabidopsis* reference genes. Due to the high number of sequences, the tree was generated with **500 bootstrap replicates** to confirm the robust grouping of paralogous pairs resulting from the recent G. max-specific 13 mya duplication event.

# Step 1. Data Collection — (*Amborella trichopoda*)

**Aim**  
To download Whole Genome, Whole Proteome, CDS, and annotations data for the basal angiosperm *Amborella trichopoda* (v1.0) downloaded from Phytozome.

---

### Why *Amborella trichopoda*?
*Amborella trichopoda* is the sole surviving species of the sister lineage to all other flowering plants. As the most basal angiosperm, it is essential for tracing the ancestral MADS-box gene states before the divergence of monocots and eudicots. The Phytozome v1.0 assembly provides a chromosome-level, well-annotated reference ideal for genome-wide searches.

---

### Tasks
- Download the complete proteome (v1.0) for domain detection and MADS-box identification.
- Retrieve the GFF3 annotation for gene model mapping.
- Obtain the genome FASTA for synteny and coordinate-level analyses.

### Databases Used
- **Phytozome (DOE JGI)** → Primary source for *Amborella trichopoda* genomic data.
  - [https://phytozome-next.jgi.doe.gov](https://phytozome-next.jgi.doe.gov)
- **Pfam** → Protein domain HMM profiles (MADS: PF00319, K-box: PF01486).
  - [https://pfam.xfam.org](https://pfam.xfam.org)

### Files Downloaded
| Data | File | Format | Purpose |
|------|------|--------|---------|
| Proteome | `Atrichopoda_291_v1.0.protein.fa` | `.fa` | Primary search database |

| Annotation | `Atrichopoda_291_v1.0.gene.gff3` | `.gff3` | Gene models & loci |

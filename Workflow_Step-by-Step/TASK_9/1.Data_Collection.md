# Step 1. Data Collection — (*Helianthus annuus*)

**Aim**  
To download Whole Genome, Whole Proteome, CDS, and annotations data for the model Asteraceae species *Helianthus annuus* (Sunflower) from Phytozome v1.2.

---

### Why *Helianthus annuus*?
*Helianthus annuus* (Sunflower) is the model species for the Asteraceae (Compositae) family, one of the most successful and diverse families of flowering plants. Sunflowers possess a complex genome that has undergone several rounds of whole-genome duplication, contributing to the development of the characteristic composite inflorescence (pseudanthium). Studying the MADS-box gene family in *H. annuus* (Phytozome v1.2) is essential for understanding how these regulators have been co-opted to control the development of ray and disc florets within the flower head.

---

### Tasks
- Identify the *Helianthus annuus* v1.2 assembly on Phytozome (DOE JGI).
- Download the complete protein dataset for genome-wide MADS-box search.
- Retrieve the corresponding GFF3 and CDS files for gene model characterization.

### Databases Used
- **Phytozome (DOE JGI)** → Primary source for *Helianthus annuus* genomic data.
  - [https://phytozome-next.jgi.doe.gov](https://phytozome-next.jgi.doe.gov)
- **Pfam** → Domain HMM profiles (PF00319, PF01486).

### File Formats
| Data | File Type | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Proteome | `Hannuus_v1.2.protein.fa` | Main search database |
| Genome | `Hannuus_v1.2.genome.fa` | Genomic mapping |
| Annotation | `Hannuus_v1.2.gene.gff3` | Site coordinates |

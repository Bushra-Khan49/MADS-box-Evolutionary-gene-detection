# Step 1. Data Collection

## Aim
To download high-quality whole genome, proteome, CDS, and annotation datasets for the water lily *Nymphaea colorata* (Nymphaeales) to investigate the early evolution of Organ identity genes.

---

## Tasks
- Identify the *Nymphaea colorata* v2.0 assembly on NCBI RefSeq.
- Download the complete protein dataset for genome-wide MADS-box search.
- Retrieve GFF3 and CDS sequences for gene model characterization.
- Cross-reference protein entries with UniProt for functional validation.

---

## Why we chose this species/version
*Nymphaea colorata* represents the Nymphaeales, one of the three lineages of the "ANA grade" (basal angiosperms). As an early-diverging lineage, it is crucial for tracing the origin and diversification of the MADS-box clades before the split of monocots and eudicots, providing a genomic snapshot of early floral evolution.

---

## Data Source
- **Database**: [NCBI RefSeq](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/refseq/) / [UniProt](https://www.uniprot.org/)
- **Assembly**: GCF_003580695.1 (v2.0)

---

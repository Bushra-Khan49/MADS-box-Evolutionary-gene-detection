> ### Step 4: Multiple Sequence Alignment (MSA)

**Aim**  
To align identified Arabidopsis MADS-box protein sequences for phylogenetic context and clade mapping.

---

### Command-Line Alignment (CLI)
```bash
cd ~/Desktop/NIPGR-data/MSA_project

# Perform alignment using MAFFT
mafft --maxiterate 1000 --localpair Arabidopsis_candidates.fa > Aligned_Arabidopsis.fa
```
[Arabidopsis MSA Alignment](https://github.com/Bushra-Khan49/MADS-box-Evolutionary-gene-detection/tree/main/Workflow_Step-by-Step/TASK_1)

---

### MEGA 12 GUI Alignment (Manual Alternative)
If you prefer a visual interface, follow these steps in **MEGA 12**:
1.  Open **MEGA 12** and click **Align** -> **Edit/Build Alignment**.
2.  Choose **Create a new alignment** -> **Protein**.
3.  Drag and drop `Arabidopsis_candidates.fa` into the explorer.
4.  Select all sequences (**Ctrl+A**) then click **Alignment** -> **Align by MUSCLE**.
5.  Save your alignment as `Aligned_Arabidopsis.meg`.

# Step 1. Data Collection

**Aim**  
To download Whole Genome, Whole Proteome, CDS, and annotations data for the Magnoliid species *Cinnamomum kanehirae* (v3) from Phytozome.

---

### Why *Cinnamomum kanehirae*?
*Cinnamomum kanehirae* (Taiwan incense cedar) is a member of the Laureales (Magnoliids), a group that diverged from other angiosperms after *Amborella* and Nymphaeales but before the split of monocots and eudicots. Magnoliids are critical for resolving the phylogenetic position and early expansion of floral MADS-box genes. The Phytozome v3 assembly provides a high-quality chromosome-level reference for this lineage.

---

### Tasks
- Download the complete proteome (v3) for domain detection and MADS-box identification.
- Retrieve the GFF3 annotation and genome FASTA for gene model mapping.
- Prepare BLAST database for genome-wide candidate screening.

### Databases Used
- **Phytozome (DOE JGI)** → Primary source for *Cinnamomum kanehirae* genomic data.
  - [https://phytozome-next.jgi.doe.gov](https://phytozome-next.jgi.doe.gov)
- **Pfam** → Protein domain HMM profiles (MADS: PF00319, K-box: PF01486).
  - [https://pfam.xfam.org](https://pfam.xfam.org)

### Files Downloaded
| Data | File | Format | Purpose |
|------|------|--------|---------|
| Proteome | `Ckanehirae_v3.0.protein_primaryTranscriptOnly.fa` | `.fa` | Primary search database |
| Genome | `Ckanehirae_v3.0.genome.fa` | `.fa` | Genomic mapping |
| Annotation | `Ckanehirae_v3.0.gene.gff3` | `.gff3` | Gene models & loci |
